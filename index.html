What it is

The Museum of Architecture is a charity dedicated to finding new ways for the public to engage with architecture and to encourage entrepreneurship within the design practice and to stimulate learning, collaboration and action.

Concept

The Museum of Architecture is a pop-up museum that doesn't have a permanent physical location, but instead hosts exhibitions, events, talks, and public installations in different venues across the city.

The School for Creative Thinkers

The School for Creative Thinkers was set up by the Museum of Architecture to teach the next generation about problem solving, design skills and innovation. These life lessons will be valuable in any profession children choose to pursue.

Children will learn new concepts, listen to stories, see examples of work done by architects and apply this learning to an in-session project that they will take home. Our courses are created and run by a team of teachers, architects and designers.
